Summary:Latitude vs Humidity

The above scatterplot measures city latitude versus humidity. We can see that the dryest locations occured in city latitudes between 60 and 80 degrees, however, the overall wide scatter of values in this plot suggests there is little to no relationship between latitude and humidity.
